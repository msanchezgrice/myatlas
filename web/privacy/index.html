Privacy Policy

Updated: Sep 8, 2025

Atlas Before & After (“the App”) is designed to process photos entirely on your device. We do not operate a backend service and we do not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account creation
  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No tracking across apps or websites

On‑Device Processing

All photos, tags, reminders, and case data remain on your device. Exports are created locally and shared only when you actively share them (e.g., via Share Sheet).

Permissions

  • Camera – capture photos.
  • Photo Library – import and optionally save exports.
  • Motion – level indicator for consistent framing.
  • Notifications – optional reminders scheduling.

Security

The app supports device‑level protections (Face ID/Touch ID) to restrict access. We recommend enabling passcode and iCloud backup encryption on your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, visit the Support page.
